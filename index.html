⚡ CRITICAL · Oveporexton Launch

⚡ HIGH RISK · Entyvio $6.8B
Spyre SPY001 — "best-in-class" α4β7 Phase 2 data (Apr 2026)
SPY001 targets the exact same mechanism as Entyvio but with potentially superior subcutaneous efficacy. SKYLINE Phase 2: −9.2 RHI, 40% remission, 51% endoscopic improvement. $403M raised at $62/share opens Phase 3 path. Any Big Pharma acquirer of Spyre becomes an instant Entyvio direct competitor.

FcRn class (argenx + UCB + Immunovant) cannibalising IVIG/SCIG
argenx at $5.2B annualised (+90% YoY), UCB RYSTIGGO growing +65%, Immunovant with $994.5M cash targeting RA/lupus/Graves'. FcRn inhibitors structurally replace immunoglobulin therapies across CIDP, gMG, ITP — the core of Takeda's PDT business. 17 consecutive growth quarters for argenx.
✓ TALENT OPPORTUNITY
~26K+ roles cut at BMS, Pfizer & Merck — active recruit window
Bristol-Myers ($2B+ restructuring, NJ sites), Pfizer (13,000+ since 2023), Merck (~6,000 planned) are collectively displacing deep oncology, hematology, commercial, and regulatory talent. Prime window to attract talent for Takeda's three upcoming launches: oveporexton, rusfertide, zasocitinib.
